About Me

Transforming brides into their most beautiful selves

Creating Magic, One Bride at a Time

With 3 years of experience in bridal makeup artistry, I specialize in creating stunning, personalized looks that enhance your natural beauty. Based in Sangli, Pune, Satara and Solapur, I'm available all over Maharashtra and have had the privilege of working with numerous beautiful brides.

My signature style blends traditional elegance with modern glamour, ensuring you look and feel absolutely radiant on your special day. From intimate ceremonies to grand destination weddings, I bring expertise, creativity, and a personal touch to every bridal transformation.

I believe every bride deserves to feel confident and beautiful. Using premium products and the latest techniques including HD and Airbrush makeup, I create flawless looks that photograph beautifully and last throughout your celebration.

Check out my Instagram @glam.by.ashwini for more stunning bridal transformations and real bride testimonials!
